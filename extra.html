Extracurriculars and Hobbies

Music

I love music, whether it's listening to or creating it. I have been playing piano for 12 years and am currently working on getting my ARCT piano performance diploma, as well as my ARCT pedagogy diploma. I also play many other instruments, including Violin, Guitar, and Drums. In my spare time I enjoy using the app Audacity to create tracks involving all four instruments.

Sports/Exercise

I enjoy doing physical activites in my free time. Every week I go to the gym 4 to 5 times. It has been a very difficult but also fun and taught me alot about how discipline and hard work pays off. The sport I am the best at is swimming. I finished the Bronze Medallion course and am continuing my journey to becoming a life guard during the summer. In addition to swimming, I enjoy Skateboarding, Snowboarding, and Skiing. Although I am not working towards anything, these are some fun activities I love doing, especially with friends.

Jobs/Volunteering

I am currently working as an English and Mathematics instructor at Kumon. I've been working there for over a year and a half and it has been a great experience. Every shift I work with over twenty students, marking their work and teaching them different math concepts like Fractions, Factoring, Geometry, etc.

During the summer, I spent three months working at a Restaurant called Mandarin. Through this job I gained valuable insights into the real world and learned a great deal about time management, financial responsibilities and the rewards of hardwork.

As I am working on my ARCT pedagogy diploma, I am obligated to teach/mentor a few piano students. For the past two years I have worked with four students and am slowly continuing to expand my piano studio. I like teaching concepts that I am passionate about and I hope that I'm able to inspire my students to enjoy playing piano rather then look at it as a burden.

In addition to my work experience, I have also volunteered at several different organizations. My favourite volunteering experience was at Sunfest, a three-day long festival hosted by TD in London. People from all over the World came to dance, sing, tell stories, or sell delicious food from their culture.
